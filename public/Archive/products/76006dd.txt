Product Name: Water softener Descaling cleaner sachets 


WEIGHT 50g Large Letter

USAGE

All water softeners


DESCRIPTION

It is recommended to clean the system with this water softener cleaner sachets at least once
a year. Specifically designed sachet to clean and descale your water softener.

Due to the special formulation of this product , it also cleans the resin, therefore
eliminates all the residues or iron and other metals that can cause contamination. Also removes
other fouling of internal valves to keep the optimum performance of your water softener.
